Our Story

Hamari shuruat ek chote se kitchen aur bahut saare sapno ke saath hui thi. Humne hamesha mana hai ki baking sirf ek kaam nahi, balki ek Art hai. Har cake jo hum banate hain, usme wahi purani family recipes aur fresh ingredients ka magic hota hai.

Aaj, CFK Bakery sirf ek naam nahi, balki hazaro celebrations ka ek hissa ban chuka hai. Hum koshish karte hain ki har bite mein aapko wahi pyaar aur mithas mile jo humne pehle din se shuru ki thi.
